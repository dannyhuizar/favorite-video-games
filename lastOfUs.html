Isn't this a show?:

Considered one of the greatest games of all time, The Last of Us has a story like no other. The gameplay, story, and characters bring an unforgettable experience that a show on HBO was made officially with actors like Pedro Pascal.

So Why Play It?:

Every time I play this game I shed a tear in some parts. I will not get into detail but the experience of this game is something that no other game can do. I don't think I have ever played a game that made me feel so many emotions as The Last Of Us. I would like to say that those who have only watched the show are missing out as the immersion that the game creates makes the emotion much stronger.

Take me back home!
