I've heard of this game once?:

The most iconic and best selling game of the first-person-shooter genre. Since 2003, Call of Duty was a game like no other during this time in the history of gaming. The fast pace full of nonstop action would appeal to such a wide audience around the world. Whether you are a gamer or not, the name Call Of Duty will ring a bell to this series whether because of controvery on the news or because of its strong community. Not only was the gameplay distinct from others, they also had a lot of variety in game modes to play that gave different objectives to play in.

So Why Play It?:

I would always see my sister play Call Of Duty when she was in high school. It would always make child me want to play but because of its content I was never allowed. However eventually, I would then play whatever game mode and always had a fun time. My personal favorite being Call of Duty, Black Ops II. I must also not forget if I got bored of shooting other players, shooting zombies was also a great experience. There was always a new location to survive the endless waves zombies provides.

Take me back home!
